Frequently Asked Questions - Ajira Platform

Ajira is an employment platform that connects casual workers with employers. We aim to provide opportunities for workers to find short-term jobs and employers to quickly find reliable help.

You can register on the Ajira platform by clicking on the "Register" button on the homepage. Follow the instructions to sign up either as a worker or employer, filling in the required details.

To post a job, log in to your employer account, go to the "Post a Job" section, and fill out the job details such as job title, description, location, and timeframe. Once posted, the job will be visible to workers on the platform.

To contact a worker or employer, navigate to the job listing page or worker profile, and use the provided contact button. This will open a message form where you can send a message through the platform.

Payment is handled directly between workers and employers. Ajira currently facilitates secure communication and job postings, but it does not process payments. We recommend using safe and reliable payment methods.
